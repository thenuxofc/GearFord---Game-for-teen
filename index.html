Gearford: The Clockwork City

A Mender's Tale, Told in Code

The great clock-city of Gearford has gone quiet. Somewhere beneath the brass rooftops, the old Engine has begun to glitch — doors won't open, gears spin backwards, sparks flicker and die. You are the newest Mender, and the only tool you carry is a small brass instruction-wheel that lets you program your own actions. Four broken districts await. Each will teach you something the Engine itself once knew.
